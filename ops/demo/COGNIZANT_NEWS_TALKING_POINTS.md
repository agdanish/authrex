# Cognizant News Talking Points — for the demo opening

**Use:** 30-second hook before slide 1. Pick ONE based on which Cognizant news the panel is freshest on.

---

## Talking point 1 — Ravi Kumar's "AI velocity gap" (RECOMMENDED — most defensible)

**The setup (verbatim):**

> *"In December last year, Cognizant CEO Ravi Kumar said five hundred billion dollars of AI infrastructure was spent in twenty-twenty-five — and most of the enterprise value is still missing. He called it the AI velocity gap.*
>
> *Today we're going to show you Authrex — built to close that gap in oncology prior auth, in ninety seconds per case."*

**Why this works:**
- It's Cognizant's CEO. Quoting them is the most respectful possible opening.
- "AI velocity gap" is now Cognizant's central 2026 narrative; every panelist has heard it.
- Frames Authrex as the *answer* to the question Ravi already posed.
- Source: [Constellation Research](https://www.constellationr.com/insights/news/cognizant-aims-solve-ai-velocity-gap)

---

## Talking point 2 — TriZetto AI Gateway launch

**The setup:**

> *"On August sixth, twenty-twenty-five, Cognizant launched TriZetto AI Gateway — described by TriZetto's CEO as 'the foundation of our agentic AI strategy.' Built on Bedrock and MCP. Today, eight months in, the catalog has zero specialty agent bundles.*
>
> *Authrex is the first one. Oncology PA, ready to deploy."*

**Why this works:**
- Names the specific Cognizant product launch.
- Identifies the gap (no specialty bundles yet) Authrex fills.
- Source: [Cognizant press release Aug 6, 2025](https://news.cognizant.com/2025-08-06-Cognizant-Debuts-TriZetto-R-AI-Gateway-to-Power-the-Next-Generation-of-AI-in-Healthcare)

## Talking point 3 — Cognizant–Anthropic partnership

**The setup:**

> *"On November fourth, twenty-twenty-five, Cognizant deployed Claude Sonnet four-six across three hundred fifty thousand employees — making them one of Anthropic's three largest customers globally.*
>
> *That's the same model Authrex's agents reason with. Same Claude. Same Bedrock. Same MCP. We didn't pick this stack — Cognizant did. We just built on it."*

**Why this works:**
- Directly aligns Authrex with Cognizant's strategic procurement decision.
- Subtle "we didn't pick this stack" is a humility move that signals you understand the customer's preferences.
- Source: [Anthropic press release](https://www.anthropic.com/news/cognizant-partnership)

## How to choose at the moment

- **If a judge mentions Ravi Kumar / "velocity gap"** → talking point 1
- **If a judge mentions TriZetto / Facets / QNXT** → talking point 2
- **If a judge mentions "stack" or "Anthropic"** → talking point 3
- **Default** → talking point 1 (broadest applicability)

## What to avoid

- Don't quote a Cognizant exec by name unless you can spell their title correctly. Ravi Kumar = CEO. Craig Mengert = TriZetto CEO. (Don't make up other names.)
- Don't paraphrase a Cognizant quote — use the exact verbatim phrasing. *"$500 billion AI infrastructure spent in 2025, but enterprise value still missing"* is the exact line.
- Don't mention Cognizant's quarterly numbers unless you've checked them in the last 7 days (they're a public company; numbers are checkable).
