## What NOT to say in the elevator

- "GenAI for healthcare" — too vague
- "We're building the future of …" — too aspirational
- "We use LLMs to …" — too implementation-focused
- "Better than X" — comparison without specifics is empty

## What MUST come out of your mouth in the elevator

1. **TriZetto AI Gateway-native** (it's their language)
2. **A dollar number** ($1,499.55 / $1.26B / pick one)
3. **The CMS-0057-F or AI velocity gap framing** (signals you understand the strategy)
